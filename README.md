# Story Builder

Jacq's plugin for building stories in Obsidian (https://obsidian.md).

This is a personal pet project that I'm making so I can use it. My main aim is organizing my own creative writing that I do for fun / as a form of art therapy. My method of organization isn't a lineart list, and instead revolves around either viewing files in a canvas or one at a time in the editor (and moving between them directly). 

> Please be aware that as of this writing, this plugin is in **EARLY** devlopment and is not ready for use/users. 

## Export?

I will **not** add an export feature added to this plugin. 

I have no interest in exporting my writing from Obsidian, and therefore will not spend any time considering such a feature. This is because I have no plans to ever publish or even share for free what I write, as like I said above, this is a form of art therapy for me. If you need such features, I recommend looking for other Obsidian plugins or consider developing your own. Any requests for export functionality will be ignored.
